# Soft Drink Bottling Volume Data

15 subgroups of size 10 containing volume measurements of soft drink
bottles. This data set is used throughout the IQCC package for
multivariate and univariate control chart examples.

## Format

A data frame with 15 lines and 10 columns.

- x1:

  The first measure.

- x2:

  The second measure.

- x3:

  The third measure.

- x4:

  The fourth measure.

- x5:

  The fifth measure.

- x6:

  The sixth measure.

- x7:

  The seventh measure.

- x8:

  The eighth measure.

- x9:

  The ninth measure.

- x10:

  The tenth measure.

## Source

Montgomery, D. C. (2009). *Introduction to Statistical Quality Control*,
6th ed. Wiley.

## Examples

``` r

data(softdrink)
```
